Economy

100 Coppers make a Silver. 10 Silvers make a Gold. Expense beyond 20 Gold are accounted for with oaths and deeds.
A worker in a city makes 1 silver a day. A farmer makes 3 Silvers in a month (~10c/day). They then pay about half in taxes.
Lots of folk will go through a whole life without seeing a Gold Coin (or traveling more than 5 or 6 miles from home).

Room & Board


Each night that you sleep in town you regain 1 lost ability point. Sleeping somewhere safe for a week regains you ALL of your lost ability points.

Each night that you are well rested allows you to make a Charisma save. Success allows you to regain two ability points. You can make this save with a -2 advantage if you are well fed.
Increase the bonus to -4 if you are Very Well Fed, and being Very Well Rested grants you the 2 ability points, and allows you to make your Charisma save for a third point.

Links to Mass Combat

Enterprises and Warbands

Enterprises can be started with a sum of 10 Gold, and have a [Risk Die] that starts at a d4.

  • At the end of a month, they will provide [risk die] in Gold back to the founding player.
  • Over the course of the month they will face a setback and, if ignored, they will lose [Risk Die] in Gold.
      • "Taking Care of" the setback prevents the loss roll.
  • Each month that an enterprise makes profit, increase the size of the [Risk Die] to the next higher size.
    Increase the scale of the enterprise in fiction.


Warbands can also be started with a sum of 10 Gold. They then require an upkeep of 6 Gold per month thereafter lest they revolt.
Equipping a warband cost 20 times the price of singular equipment.

  • Warbands start at d6 hp, 8 8 8, d4 pitchforks (b)
  • They can Level after [level #] successful missions.
    • For example, your pikemen have completed three adventures and are level 3. They need to successfully complete three more missions before leveling to 4, then four more to level to 5
  • Each Level grants +d6 hp, test each ability score to improve

Warbands attacking individual creatures inflict Ed10 damage to them, while damage coming in from individuals is Impaired, unless it is has the blast tag.
