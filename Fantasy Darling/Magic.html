Magic

Magic is powerful and fickle. The most folk mistrusts it, and will shun the use of it publicly.
You need to find somebody to train you in the arts, and most are not willing to share their knowledge.

Casting

Spells are stored in spell books, wands and scrolls. You need the source in your hand to be able to cast.
You decide how many Magic Dice (MD) to invest in the casting, and roll them.
Each spell will have effects based on either how many die are rolled [die], or the sum of all die rolled [sum].
Dice that roll 1-3 are returned, while dice that roll 4-6 are lost for the day.
More dice invested grants a stronger spell, with a higher chance for spell burn (doubles) or doom (triples).

Magical Gear

Potions: Drink for Effect
Scrolls: Read for Effect, Roll MD, always spent.
Rod: Holds 1 or 2 spells, and +1 MD

Magic User Gear: Gain +1 MD for each (Staff, Robes, Pointy Hat)
Cleric Gear: Gain +1 MD for each (Icon, Weapon, Shield) Each needs 'blessing' of church + symbol engraved (10g donation)

Rings: Gain the effect by wearing. Max 2x. Amulet: Gain effect from wearing. One only.
