Action !Lord will ride out with goons and demand a joust. Winning grants hospitality for a month, losing or declining they demand your armour, or tribute. Despot will ride out and attempt to slay the PCs to loot them, else hide and prep for a siege if the PCs are tough. Patriarch will ride out and demand a tithe (1/8 the PCs wealth) else request a quest. Tithe or quest grants 1 month hospitality. High Priest will ride out and demand a tithe (^) else a quest. Refusal of either prompts an attack. Wizard will demand a magical item, refusal risks a foul spell or trick. Necromancer will demand a magical item, refusal risks attack, curse, or geas to quest for one.
