Monster Creation

When creating a monster, or bringing in from another system, keep an eye on these guidelines:

  • Really get a feel for the monster. What makes them unique, and how to they affect the characters?
  • Get a picture of them in your head. What do they smell like?

Abilities

Creatures have the 3 base abilities, Strength, Dexterity, Charisma. Just like people, 10 is the average score.
15 is pretty damn good, while 6 is pretty crappy. They both top/bottom out at 18/3. Supernatural may hit 19 or 20.

hp and armour

How 'fight-ey' is the monster? Rough and tumble? 5hp. Tough as nails, or a boss? 8-12hp
Having more than 12 hp can turn the fight into a slog.

Does it have a shield, or cool duds? 1a
Chain mail, or high speed? 2a
Is it literally made of armour? 3a

Damage

find a comfortable spot between a sword strike -d6, or naval artillery +d12
Can it hit a few people at once? give it burst damage for explosions, weapon sweeps, or machine guns.
Does it have multiple attacks, or can it attack twice in one turn? Give it a couple attack dice.
If it's a solo creature, let it hit two separate targets each round.

Quirks

Does it have a nasty finishing move? Make it happen on Critical Damage (CD)
Does it have special immunities or resistances? Make those damage sources Impaired, or completely ignored.
Does it have other weird quirks? Just make them happen in your description.
Default to not giving saves to prevent the nasty shit. Less chances for nothing happening.
